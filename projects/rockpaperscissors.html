Live Site

Rock, Paper, Scissors

Client/Course:

WEB 2630 Client-Side Frameworks

Technologies:

HTML5, CSS3, BootStrap, vanilla JavaScript, and Vue.

Description:

I made this game in my Client-Side Frameworks class. The game allows you to play 10 rounds of rock, paper, scissors against the computer.

Because the focus of this class was on Vue, I later had to go in and improve the look of the site before including it in my portfolio. I chose to change the front-end framework from Foundation to Bootstrap.

Challenges:

Changing the front-end framework from Foundation to Bootstrap was labor and time intensive since so many of the styles are in-line.
