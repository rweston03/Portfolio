Heidelberg Veterinary Clinic

Client/Course:

WEB 2890 Client-Side Portfolio

Technologies:

HTML5, CSS3, BootStrap, and Sass.

Description:

I made this site in my Client-Side Portfolio class. I chose a project off of 99Designs.com and fulfilled the specifications for the project. I created a mood board and personas for the site, did a content audit of the existing business site, and created a medium-fidelity mockup in Adobe XD prior to coding the site. I also had three friends and my instructor provide feedback on the site. I then went through the site and addressed their concerns to improve the usability and feel of the site.

Challenges:

I had difficulty making all the BootStrap cards the same size when resizing the screen. I learned that the BootStrap "row" class doesn't play well with the "flex-fill" class, so I had to rewrite that section in order to get it to work.

Resources:

FontAwesome Icons, Google Fonts, existing Heidelberg Veterinary Clinic logo, pictures from Pexels and Pixabay, and Hover.css.
